About

Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Sathya, I'm a ML researcher who loves exploring how computers can understand and work with different types of information - whether it's text, images, or speech.
I enjoy solving challenging problems and collaborating with people who share my curiosity. To be a bit more technical, I like working on multimodal multilingual machine learning models, evaluating, and improving their performance.

Originally from Chennai, India and currently based in Pittsburgh, I spend my days at Carnegie Mellon University working on interesting projects in artificial intelligence.

When I'm not working on projects, you'll probably find me on the cricket field or playing badminton. I'm also a huge movie buff and pride myself on enjoying both critically-acclaimed masterpieces and what others might call "terrible" movies - because sometimes those are the most fun! I believe there's charm in both a well-crafted film and a so-bad-it's-good movie marathon.
